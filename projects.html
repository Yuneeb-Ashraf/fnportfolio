AVL Project

HIL ADAPTER BOARD for CELL TESTER 600A

HIL adapter Board exists b/w Digital & Analog Board to provide interface for signal conditioning that can be derived (simulated) from Hardware in Loop (HIL).

Cell Tester 600A is a bidirectional multichannel DC power supply that tests, characterizes and validates battery cells requiring high current. It acheives an output current of +- 600A up to 5V at the cell tabs.

A battery cell is the most important component in an electric powertrain in terms of cost and performance.

To ensure reliable and robust battery packs for electric vehicles, it is essential to master the electrochemical processes occuring within the cell.
